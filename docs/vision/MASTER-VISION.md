# Master Vision

AI Drama Factory is an AI Production Operating System that transforms a
creative brief into audiovisual content through planning, asset management,
generative AI, review, editorial, audio, quality control, and reproducible
publishing.

We are not building the best AI model. We are building a production studio
that can use the best available model, compensate for its weaknesses through
workflow, and replace it when better technology appears.

## Scope

Drama is the hardest quality benchmark because it demands convincing acting,
continuity, pacing, sound, and editorial judgment. The system must also serve
film, short drama, advertising, corporate media, music video, animation,
social-media content, and educational content.

## Principles

1. A model is a worker, not the system.
2. A shot is the primary production unit.
3. Work artifacts are mutable; published artifacts are immutable.
4. Human approval remains authoritative.
5. Every output must be reproducible.
6. Quality is evaluated at raw-shot and finished-sequence levels.
7. Expensive inference follows approved planning.
8. Production and research workflows are separated.
9. Technology adoption is evidence-driven.
10. Architecture follows capability, not vendor.
