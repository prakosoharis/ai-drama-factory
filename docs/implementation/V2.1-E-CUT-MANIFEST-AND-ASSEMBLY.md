# V2.1-E Cut Manifest and Assembly

V2.1-E MVP makes the Cut Manifest the editorial source of truth. A Selection
chooses a preferred Candidate; a Cut Manifest places that Candidate in timeline
order with explicit trims; assembly follows the manifest rather than scanning
directories or guessing filenames.

Cuts are versioned. Cloning a Cut creates a new manifest with `parent_cut_id`,
leaving the parent unchanged. Mock assembly creates deterministic text output,
not video. Real FFmpeg assembly is deferred until real video Candidates exist.

Advanced picture lock, concurrent allocation, complex editing, transitions,
recovery hardening, and real-media conform are V2.2 backlog.
