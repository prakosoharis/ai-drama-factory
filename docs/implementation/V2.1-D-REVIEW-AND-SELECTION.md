# V2.1-D Review and Candidate Selection

## MVP status

V2.1-D is complete for MVP. It supports adding Reviews and Findings, human
picture approval, Candidate rejection, comparison, ROUGH_CUT selection,
effective review status, immutable Candidate packages, and file/checksum
validation. Approval and Selection are separate records.

A Candidate is the generated result. A Review is an assessment. A Finding is a
specific issue. Rejection preserves the Candidate. Reviews and Selections are
append-only; a newer Selection may reference an older Selection it supersedes.
Only a human may grant final picture approval.

V2.2 Hardening backlog: concurrent Review/Selection allocation, full waiver
edge cases, complex supersession validation/cycle detection, and expanded
exhaustive tests. Cut Manifest and assembly are V2.1-E work and have not
started.
