# ADR-000: Project Principles

## Decision

AI Drama Factory follows the principles in the master vision: models are
replaceable workers; shots are the production unit; human approval is
authoritative; work and published artifacts have distinct mutability; outputs
are reproducible; expensive inference follows approved planning; quality is
evaluated at shot and sequence level; research is separate from production;
technology adoption is evidence-driven; and architecture is capability-driven,
not vendor-driven.

## Consequences

The system will favour explicit production records and small, reviewable
contracts over hidden prompt-only state or renderer-specific workflows.
